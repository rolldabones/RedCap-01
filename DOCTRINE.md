# RedCap-01 Doctrine

**Version:** 1.0.0

**Repository:** RedCap-01

**Method:** Objective-to-Risk Alignment Check

---

## Purpose

RedCap-01 exists to determine whether Enterprise Risk Management (ERM) improves the decisions that determine whether organizational objectives are achieved.

It is intentionally narrow.

It is not an ERM framework.

It is not a maturity model.

It is a practical diagnostic.

---

## Governing Question

> **Does Enterprise Risk Management improve the decisions that determine whether organizational objectives are achieved?**

Every assessment, template, prompt and recommendation in this repository should contribute to answering that question.

---

## The Doctrine

### 1. Objectives precede risks.

Risk has meaning only in relation to an objective.

Begin every assessment by understanding what the organization is trying to achieve.

---

### 2. Strategy and risk are inseparable.

Every strategic choice creates uncertainty.

ERM should illuminate that uncertainty before options disappear.

---

### 3. ERM exists to improve decisions.

The purpose of ERM is not to produce documentation.

Its purpose is to improve the quality, speed, resilience and accountability of important organizational decisions.

---

### 4. Uncertainty should be explicit.

Identify assumptions, scenarios and dependencies openly.

Avoid false precision when uncertainty cannot reasonably be reduced.

---

### 5. Risk appetite should guide action.

Risk appetite becomes useful only when translated into practical decision boundaries that leaders can apply consistently.

---

### 6. Triggers should precede surprises.

Observable triggers should prompt reassessment, escalation, pause or pivot before uncertainty becomes failure.

---

### 7. Ownership should always be explicit.

Objectives, decisions, assumptions, boundaries, triggers and actions should each have clear ownership.

Governance without ownership is incomplete.

---

### 8. Governance should live where decisions are made.

ERM should be embedded within strategy, planning, investment, transformation, operations and performance reviews.

Parallel reporting processes rarely change outcomes.

---

### 9. Value should be observable.

ERM demonstrates value through decisions improved, opportunities enabled, losses reduced, earlier pivots and stronger governance.

Activity alone is not evidence of effectiveness.

---

### 10. Learning completes the governance cycle.

Every important decision should improve future objectives, assumptions, boundaries and governance.

Continuous learning is a governance capability.

---

## Design Commitments

RedCap-01 will remain:

- objective-first
- decision-led
- evidence-oriented
- practical
- explainable
- auditable
- conservative about uncertainty
- lightweight
- complementary to the GRCnext™ ecosystem

Future versions should strengthen these characteristics rather than expand the repository's scope.

---

## Relationship to Other Repositories

RedCap-01 complements, but does not replace:

- **GRC Workbook**, which provides a comprehensive Governance, Risk Management and Compliance implementation method.
- **Risk-Informed Decision Making and Continuous Risk Management**, which structures individual decisions under uncertainty.
- **RedCap-00**, which evaluates operational optionality under disruption.

RedCap-01 contributes one capability:

> Determining whether Enterprise Risk Management is genuinely anchored to objectives, decisions and operational execution.

---

## Intellectual Lineage

RedCap-01 is inspired by the practical orientation of:

Ryan Luttenton, Stefany Samp and Alexa Stone,

*From Guidance to Action: Exploring Practical Enterprise Risk Management*,

Committee of Sponsoring Organizations of the Treadway Commission (COSO), 2026.

It operationalizes these ideas within the GRCnext™ ecosystem while remaining an independent method.

---

## Closing Principle

> **Good Enterprise Risk Management does not begin with risks. It begins with objectives and succeeds only when it improves the decisions that determine whether those objectives are achieved.**

**Final Liability rests with the Human.**
