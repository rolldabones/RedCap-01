# RedCap-01 Trigger Register

A Trigger Register translates uncertainty into observable events that require reassessment or action.

It is designed to answer one question:

> **What observable event would cause us to reconsider this decision?**

## Trigger Design Checklist

Each trigger should be:

- Observable
- Objective where practical
- Timely
- Owned
- Linked to a specific decision
- Paired with a predefined action
- Reviewed regularly

Avoid triggers that merely request additional reporting.

---

## Review Questions

1. Is the trigger linked to a strategic objective?
2. Is the affected decision identified?
3. Is the threshold understandable?
4. Can the owner monitor it?
5. Is the required action already agreed?
6. Does the trigger preserve options by prompting early action?

---

## Notes

Unknown or unsupported assumptions should be identified explicitly.

Triggers should evolve as objectives, strategy and operating conditions change.
